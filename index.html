About Me

I'm a software engineer turned AI researcher, driven by a curiosity for how intelligent systems can solve real-world problems responsibly.

I'm a researcher and AI engineer working at the intersection of language model safety, multilingual NLP, and AI for digital health. I'm completing a Master of Information Technology (Artificial Intelligence) at Macquarie University, Sydney, building on a Computer Science background and industry experience as a software engineer at BRAC Bank PLC.

My recent work includes MMOR-Bench, a multilingual benchmark I am developing to evaluate safety alignment and over-refusal across frontier LLMs and seven languages — revealing how safety-aligned models systematically disadvantage low-resource language speakers. Alongside this, I'm building a research agenda around culturally grounded AI safety for mental health support in diverse populations. I believe responsible AI demands evaluation frameworks that reflect the full breadth of human language and lived experience.
